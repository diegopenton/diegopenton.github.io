About Me

My name is Diego Penton, and I am pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science with a concentration in Artificial Intelligence at Florida Polytechnic University. My academic journey has allowed me to develop strong skills in C, C++, SQL, and Python, which I’ve applied to projects in data modeling, algorithms, and AI-driven systems.

Currently, I’m developing a Modular Algorithmic Trading Framework, a project that explores trading strategies through modular “branch” models like RSI, GARCH, and machine learning. My goal is to improve backtesting reliability, reduce overfitting, and strengthen risk management metrics such as the Sharpe ratio and Value-at-Risk. This work is inspired by both my studies and my personal interest in the stock market, where I see a unique opportunity to merge computer science and finance.

Looking ahead, I’m passionate about pursuing a career in quantitative finance and algorithmic trading, with the long-term goal of earning a master’s degree in machine learning and contributing to research in neural networks and advanced financial modeling. Beyond my coursework, I’ve had the opportunity to collaborate on diverse projects — from database systems to graph-based optimization — which continue to fuel my drive to solve complex, real-world problems at the intersection of AI and finance
